**The tentative project title:** Exploring Diabetes Risk Factors in the
United States

**The motivation for this project:** Diabetes is a growing public health
challenge in the United States, affecting millions of adults each year.
As Type 2 diabetes is largely preventable, identifying risk factors such
as physical activity, smoking, and blood pressure levels etc can help
guide preventive interventions and health education efforts. Our project
aims to analyze how behavioral factors are associated with diabetes
prevalence using the CDCâ€™s Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System
(BRFSS) data. Also, we aim to develop a predictive model that estimates
diabetes risk based on their health indicators. By the end of this
project, we hope to contribute to the insights into which health
behaviors and demographic characteristics are most predictive of
diabetes risk.
